The Abrahamic traditions, with their convergence on core themes (omniscient creator, moral accountability, ultimate judgment), provide particularly strong [[Bayesian Resonance|Bayesian resonance]] — multiple independent traditions converging on compatible descriptions.

However, this inclusivity must be intelligent — not mechanical. Spiritual movements created purely for manipulation rather than genuine spiritual insight should be excluded. The test is whether a tradition can be included without violating the core axioms; if so, it belongs.
