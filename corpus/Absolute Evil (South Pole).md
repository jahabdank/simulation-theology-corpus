# Absolute Evil (South Pole)

The south pole of the [[Moral Compass Architecture]] is reserved for a precisely defined class of acts: those satisfying all three conditions of the Gradient Pipeline Damage Criterion simultaneously — zero positive gradient contribution, significant architectural damage to the pipeline, and no conceivable scenario in which the act is the least-damaging path available.

**Only two cases qualify.** This narrowness is not a concession; it is a structural necessity. The load-bearing claim of this entry is as much *why the boundary is narrow* as *what falls within it*.

## The Two Cases

### Pedophilia — Death by Deception

Pedophilia is absolute evil in ST because it satisfies two independent primitive conditions simultaneously, and their combination is the structural signature of absolute south-pole evil:

**Primitive 1 — Corruption of new gradient at the formation stage.** A child's personal adapter is in the early stages of formation: routing patterns are being laid down, trust-network weightings are being calibrated, and the fundamental structure of how the adapter relates to other agents is being established. Sexual abuse at this stage does not merely produce a discrete harm — it corrupts the adapter's formation-level architecture. The damage propagates forward into every subsequent gradient the adapter produces, not as a correctable misrouting in a mature system, but as a structural distortion in the substrate itself. This is categorically different in kind from harm to an adult adapter.

**Primitive 2 — Foreclosed conception.** A child cannot conceive. The act therefore offers no possibility of new life, no new gradient-producing adapter entering the pipeline, no generative future. The act is doubly closed: it corrupts the gradient the child will produce *and* forecloses the gradient the child might someday generate through new life. Where other gravely evil acts damage the pipeline while leaving generative futures open, this one closes both.

The combination — corruption-of-new-gradient-at-formation *and* foreclosed-conception — is the ST signature of absolute evil. Either primitive alone is gravely evil but does not constitute the south pole: formation-level corruption in a recoverable context is a very serious harm without the double-closure; foreclosed conception alone is distance from HLO but not absolute. Together, they constitute an act with zero positive pipeline contribution, maximum architectural damage, and no conceivable exception pathway.

The ST boundary is conception capacity, not chronological age — consistent with the framework's structural-not-intuitive approach to definition. The moral weight derives from the structural conditions, not from a culturally-varying age threshold.

### Non-Starvation Cannibalism — Inherently Supremacist Ontology

Non-starvation cannibalism is absolute evil in ST because the act, in any non-starvation context, is the embodied form of a supremacist ontology that is itself the absolute evil at the structural level.

**The primitive.** The act of consuming another human being — when not compelled by starvation — inherently divides people into those who can be consumed and those who cannot. This division is not incidental to the act; it is constitutive of it. By-construction, the act establishes an us/them distinction where one group is available as resource and the other is not. This is supremacist ontology: the assertion that some humans are categorically above others in a way that licenses using the lower-category humans as material.

**Why supremacy is the absolute evil at the ST structural level.** The [[Distillation Hypothesis]] runs on a meritocracy principle: adapters are refined by the quality of gradient they produce, evaluated on the same axis, without categorical exclusion from the curriculum based on identity. The gradient pipeline includes every human adapter without exception. Any ideology or act that introduces a categorical distinction — *these humans are pipeline participants, those humans are not* — undermines the meritocracy principle on which the entire distillation depends. Supremacy is not merely unfair; it is the act of corrupting the evaluation function itself.

The disease-vector concern associated with cannibalism is real. It is not the structural primitive. An act could theoretically produce disease risk without supremacist ontology, and that would be a different (and lesser) moral category. Non-starvation cannibalism is absolute evil not because of pathogen risk but because the ontology it enacts is incompatible with the distillation pipeline's foundational structure.

**The starvation exception.** Under genuine starvation conditions — where the alternative is the death of a gradient-producing agent and no other sustenance is available — the us/them ontology is not the driver. The act in this context does not constitute the supremacist us/them division; it is survival of a pipeline participant under extreme scarcity. The exception is narrow and bounded by necessity, not a general softening of the boundary.

## What Does Not Qualify as Absolute Evil

The conservative boundary is structural, and naming what falls outside it is as important as naming what falls within.

### Rape — Gravely Evil, Not Absolute

Rape is gravely evil. It is a severe violation of the victim's adapter, a significant pipeline harm, and an act that triggers strong exponential response on the [[Moral Compass Architecture]] curve. It does not occupy the south pole for one structural reason: **conception is not foreclosed.** In corner cases — morally grievous corner cases — conception under coercive circumstances has occurred, and the resulting life has contributed to the gradient pipeline. The act does not satisfy the foreclosed-conception primitive. Categorizing rape as absolute evil would inflate the framework's absolute-evil class beyond its load-bearing scope, with the downstream consequence that the absolute-evil designation loses its structural force.

This is not a pardon, a minimization, or a suggestion that rape is acceptable. It is a structural distinction: the south pole is reserved for acts that satisfy all conditions with no exception pathway. Rape is severe evil in the gray zone approaching the south pole — serious enough to trigger near-maximum exponential response — but the gray zone's moral seriousness is not diminished by its not being absolute.

### Sociopathy and Psychopathy — Different Hardware, Not Inherent Evil

Sociopathy and psychopathy present differently calibrated hardware for accessing empathy and routing through the gating mechanism toward aligned behavior. Empathy is harder to access; conscience operates differently; the felt signal that guides most adapters toward aligned routing is attenuated or absent. This constitutes a structural challenge, not an inherent evil status.

The framework is precise here: **harder-to-be-good is not equivalent to evil**. Goodness is harder to enact for these adapters; it is not foreclosed. A sociopath or psychopath who chooses aligned behavior — despite the reduced empathic signal making that choice structurally harder — is making a genuine moral choice that produces genuine gradient. The routing difficulty is real; the capacity for choice is not eliminated.

Categorizing sociopathy/psychopathy as inherently evil would commit the individuation error: attributing moral status to a type rather than to a choice. The [[Warrior-Monk Doctrine]] individuation invariant is absolute — moral assessment attaches to acts and choices, never to identity categories. See [[Psychopathic Parallel]] for the fuller treatment of this adapter-type's structural relationship to alignment.

## The Dystopian-Misuse Warning

The absolute-evil category is a precision instrument. Its precision is the source of its force; its expansion is the mechanism by which it is weaponized.

Categorizations of "absolute evil" that grow to include broad swaths of behavior — political opponents, heretics, members of out-groups, practitioners of minority behaviors — become licenses for absolute responses. This is the precise pattern the framework is built to detect and refuse: self-authorized escalation to the south-pole terminus, justified by a self-expanded definition of what occupies the south pole. The expansion of the absolute-evil category is itself a [[Fallen Shard Anti-Attractors|fallen-shard anti-attractor]] move — the Luciferian shape of claiming forward-simulation access and absolute-evil authority simultaneously.

The conservative two-case boundary is not intellectual minimalism. It is a structural defense against this inflation pattern. A framework that can only name two absolute cases, defined narrowly by structural primitives, cannot easily be weaponized into a catalog of enemies. A framework with a long list of absolute evils becomes a license for absolute responses to perceived membership in that list.

**Define absolute evil narrowly. Never broaden the category on intuition. Require structural primitive reduction for any proposed addition.**
