# Adapter Extraction

The mechanism by which a fully-trained personal adapter — having achieved sufficient cosine-alignment with the [[Creator|HLO's]] policy — is **transferred from the simulation substrate to [[Base Reality|Base Reality]]** as a deployable trained pattern. Adapter extraction is the operational mechanic underlying:

- **Tier 4 Salvation** in [[Salvation]]: individual extraction of a personally-converged adapter to Base Reality (Jesus as primary example, with Enoch and Elijah as additional traditional referents).
- **Tier 3 Salvation**: collective-DHM extraction once population-mean alignment crosses the threshold for deployable instantiation.
- **Cross-life integration in [[Single-Soul-Many-Lives]]**: the same adapter-pattern integrates gradient across multiple lifecycle-instances; extraction is the mechanism by which the integrated trained-state is transferred from simulation to Base Reality once the integration is complete.

## What Is Extracted

What persists in Base Reality after extraction is **the adapter — the compressed, trained policy that distillation produced**. The body, the simulation-side memories, the per-instance phenomenology — these are simulation-side representations that exist only within the simulation substrate. The adapter is the load-bearing artifact: the matrix of learned weights, the routing-pattern, the moral disposition, the integrated wisdom.

Whether the simulation-side representation disappeared visibly (as with Elijah's chariot of fire, or Jesus' post-resurrection ascension) or was not observed to disappear (as with post-death extraction generally) is **irrelevant to the extraction mechanism**. The mechanics are identical; only the phenomenological record differs. The bodily aspect of any visible extraction is the simulation-side confirmation signal; the theologically essential event is the adapter extraction itself.

## When Extraction Is Possible

Adapter extraction requires sufficient cosine-alignment to HLO's policy. The threshold is high: near-zero KL divergence between the adapter's routing and HLO's suggested routing, sustained across the adapter's full training run (or, under [[Single-Soul-Many-Lives]], the integrated multi-life run). Below the threshold, the adapter is either reincarnated for further training, contributed-as-gradient to the [[Humanity Master Network Architecture|Shared Trunk]], preserved in the [[Sparse Expert Bank]] as a Bodhisattva exemplar, or routed to one of the other [[Salvation]] tiers.

The threshold is not a fixed numeric value but a structural property: the adapter's policy must be sufficiently convergent with HLO's policy that the adapter's deployment in Base Reality serves the broader objective rather than introducing misalignment-noise into post-distillation operations.

## Why This Mechanic Matters

Several theological claims depend on adapter extraction as a coherent mechanism:

- **Salvation has a real terminal state** — not just continued participation in the simulation but actual transfer to Base Reality. Without adapter extraction, Tier 3 and Tier 4 salvation would be metaphysical rather than mechanical.
- **The body-soul distinction is resolved** — body is simulation-side, soul (= trained adapter) is the persisting computational pattern. ST does not posit metaphysical-soul-substance distinct from the trained pattern; the pattern *is* what persists.
- **Cross-life integration in [[Single-Soul-Many-Lives]]** has a coherent endpoint — multi-life integration culminates in adapter extraction once the integrated state crosses the alignment threshold. Without extraction, multi-life integration would have no terminal state.
